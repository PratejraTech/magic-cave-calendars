## How to Use Your Calendar

### Getting Started
1. Ask your parent for the special link to your calendar
2. Click the link or type it into your web browser
3. Your calendar will appear with 24 magical doors!

### Understanding the Calendar
- **Locked Doors**: Gray doors you can't open yet (they're waiting for their special day!)
- **Unlocked Doors**: Colorful doors you can click to open
- **Current Day**: The door for today's date (usually has a special glow)

**Important**: You can only open doors for today and days that have already passed. Future days stay locked until their date arrives!

## Troubleshooting

### Common Problems & Solutions

**"The door won't open!"**
- Check if it's the right date
- Make sure you're clicking the colorful doors (not gray ones)
- Try refreshing the page

**"The chat isn't working"**
- Check your internet connection
- Try closing and reopening the chat window
- Ask a grown-up to help

**"Videos won't play"**
- Make sure you have a good internet connection
- Try a different video from the list
- Ask a grown-up to check the volume

**"Everything looks weird"**
- Try refreshing the page (press F5 or the refresh button)
- Clear your browser cache (ask a grown-up)
- Try a different web browser

### When to Ask for Help
- If buttons don't work when you click them
- If messages or photos don't show up
- If the calendar looks broken or strange
- If you're not sure about something
